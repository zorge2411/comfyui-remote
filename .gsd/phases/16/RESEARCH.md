# Research: Phase 16 - Icon Refinement

## Objective

Create 5 distinct, premium icon concepts for the ComfyUI Remote app that align with the "Rich Aesthetics" and "Premium" design goals.

## Current State

- **Format**: Adaptive Icon (XML vector).
- **Style**: Tech/Cyberpunk. Green (#00FF88) 'C' shape made of nodes on a dark background.
- **Constraints**: Must be legible as a small icon. Must fit the adaptive icon circle/squircle.

## Concepts

### Concept 1: "Neon Flux"

- **Description**: A vibrant, glowing node network forming a subtle 'C' shape.
- **Style**: Cyberpunk, high contrast, neon gradients (Purple/Blue/Green).
- **Vibe**: Tech-savvy, powerful, modern.

### Concept 2: "Glass Flow"

- **Description**: Abstract 3D glass layers representing data flow.
- **Style**: Glassmorphism, soft gradients, transparency, depth.
- **Vibe**: Premium, clean, sophisticated.

### Concept 3: "Minimal Node"

- **Description**: A simplified, flat geometric abstraction of a node connection.
- **Style**: Minimalist, Swiss design, flat colors (Dark Grey + Accent).
- **Vibe**: Professional, reliable, tool-like.

### Concept 4: "Comfy Gradient"

- **Description**: A soft, organic shape using the official ComfyUI color palette (if distinct) or a rich, warm gradient.
- **Style**: Soft UI, organic curves, rich gradient mesh.
- **Vibe**: Friendly, accessible, artistic.

### Concept 5: "Digital Canvas"

- **Description**: A representation of an image being generated or a pixel grid transforming into art.
- **Style**: Digital art, pixels vs. vectors, creative.
- **Vibe**: Creative, generative, magical.
